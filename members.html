Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

To become a member, simply click the "Sign Up" button above and complete the registration form. After submitting your information, you'll receive a confirmation email with further instructions.

Is there a fee to join?

No, membership is completely free for all regular attendees of Rhema Calvary Center.

What if I forget my password?

If you forget your password, simply click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. You'll receive an email with instructions to reset your password.

Can I update my personal information?

Yes, once logged in, you can update your personal information, communication preferences, and privacy settings from your profile page.

How do I join a small group?

After logging in, navigate to the "Groups" section where you can browse available small groups based on interests, location, or meeting times. Click "Join Group" to request membership.
